WHY 4 TURNS = ORIGINAL DIRECTION
=================================

In the main DFS loop, we turn right 4 times:

Start:    Facing direction D
Turn 1:   Facing (D+1) mod 4
Turn 2:   Facing (D+2) mod 4
Turn 3:   Facing (D+3) mod 4
Turn 4:   Facing (D+4) mod 4 = D  â† Back to original!

Example: Start facing UP (0)
Turn 1:   facing RIGHT (1)
Turn 2:   facing DOWN (2)
Turn 3:   facing LEFT (3)
Turn 4:   facing UP (0) â† Original direction!

This is why the algorithm works - after trying all 4 directions,
robot is automatically facing the original direction.
